Meat Loaf

Main Dish

6 servings

This recipe is anything but regular old meatloaf! Everyone will love this moist version made in the slow cooker, with milk, mushrooms, and a little sage for extra flavour.

Instructions

Combine eggs, milk, bread crumbs, onion, salt, sage, and mushrooms in a large bowl. Crumble ground beef over mixture and stir well to combine. Shape into a round loaf; place in a 5-quart slow cooker. Cover and cook on Low until a meat thermometer reads 160 degrees F (71 degrees C), 5 to 6 hours.

Whisk ketchup, brown sugar, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce in a small bowl; spoon sauce over meat loaf. Return to slow cooker and cook on Low until heated through, about 15 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before cutting.

Tips and Suggestions

Add a touch of sriracha hot sauce if you want to give it that extra kick.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
    1.5 pounds of ground beef
  • Egg
    2 eggs
  • Milk
    3/4 cups of milk
  • Seasoned Bread Crumbs
    2/3 cups of seasoned bread crumbs
  • Dried Minced Onions
    2 teaspoons of dried minced onions
  • Salt
    1 teaspoon of salt
  • Rubbed Sage
    1/2 teaspoons of rubbed sage
  • Mushrooms
    1/2 cups of sliced fresh mushrooms
  • Ketchup
    1/4 cups of ketchup
  • Brown Sugar
    2 tablespoons of brown sugar
